Loot of the Forest

An entry for Ludum Dare 28 - You Only Get One (of each item)

Bribe the forest guardians and make your way through their maze.
You only have one item per species of guardian, so use them wisely.
Cliffs can only be traversed from the top.
Some planning will be required to come out with all the treasure and make it to the next maze.
Move with WSAD. Undo with U. Restart the current level with R.
